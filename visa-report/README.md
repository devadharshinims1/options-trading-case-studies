
# Visa (V) Options Trading Case Study

## Strategy: Long Straddle

## Objective
To profit from a potential large price movement in Visa without predicting direction.

---

## Company Overview
Visa Inc. is a global payment technology company enabling digital transactions worldwide.

---

## Pre-Trade Analysis

### Fundamental Analysis
- Revenue: $40B (+11% YoY)
- Net Income: $20.1B
- Net Margin: 50%
- Strong cash flow and shareholder returns

Conclusion: Financially strong and stable company

---

### Technical Analysis
- Price: ~$340
- RSI: 43 (slightly oversold)
- Support: $338
- Resistance: $353

Conclusion: Range-bound, potential breakout

---

## Strategy Setup

- Strategy: Long Straddle
- Strike Price: $345
- Expiry: Nov 28, 2025

| Position | Contracts | Cost |
|----------|----------|------|
| Call     | 3        | $1,155 |
| Put      | 3        | $3,675 |

**Total Cost:** $4,830  

**Breakeven:**  
- Upper: $361.10  
- Lower: $328.90  

---

## Outcome

- Final Price: $323.77  
- Profit: **$1,539**

---

## Key Insights

- Strategy worked due to downward movement
- Regulatory news triggered volatility
- Strong fundamentals limited extreme movement

---

## Lessons Learned

- Straddles require **large price movement**
- Expensive strategy if volatility is low
- Works best during major events

---

## Skills Demonstrated

- Options strategy selection  
- Technical + fundamental analysis  
- Risk management

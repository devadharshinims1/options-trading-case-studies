
# 📉 NVIDIA (NVDA) Options Trading Case Study

## Strategy: Bull Call Spread

## Objective
To profit from a moderate upward movement in NVIDIA stock with limited risk.

---

## Company Overview
NVIDIA is a leader in AI and GPU computing, with strong growth driven by data centers.

---

## Pre-Trade Analysis

### Fundamental Analysis
- Revenue: $130.5B (+114% YoY)
- Net Income: $72.9B
- ROE: 92%
- Strong AI-driven growth

Conclusion: Strong long-term fundamentals

---

### Technical Analysis
- Price trend: Strong uptrend
- RSI: 78 (overbought)
- Support: $175
- Resistance: $198

Conclusion: Bullish but slightly overextended

---

## Strategy Setup

- Strategy: Bull Call Spread
- Buy Call: $190  
- Sell Call: $210  
- Expiry: Nov 28, 2025  

**Total Cost:** $3,795  
**Breakeven:** $202.65  

**Max Profit:** $2,205  
**Max Loss:** $3,795  

---

## Outcome

- Final Price: ~$177  
- Result: **Max Loss ($3,795)**  

---

## Key Insights

- Trade failed due to downward movement
- External factors (regulation, demand concerns) impacted price

---

## Lessons Learned

- Bull spreads require strong bullish confirmation
- Momentum matters more than fundamentals short-term
- Risk-defined strategies can still lose fully

---

## Skills Demonstrated

- Options spread strategies  
- Market trend evaluation  
- Risk-reward analysis
